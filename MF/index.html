Indian Mutual Funds SchemeNames

What are mutual funds? A mutual fund is a company that pools money from many investors and invests the money in securities such as stocks, bonds, and short-term debt. The combined holdings of the mutual fund are known as its portfolio.
In India, the Mutual Fund concept emerged in 1960. The first open ended Mutual Fund was created as public sector enterprise with the establishment of Unit Trust of India in 1964 under the Central Legislation, i.e., Unit Trust of India Act-1963.
